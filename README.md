## Northwind Traders: Sales & Operational Analysis

## Project Overview
This project delivers a diagnostic evaluation of Northwind Traders' global sales operations across three years (2013–2015). 
Using Power BI for advanced visualization and DAX for revenue calculations, the analysis investigates a $474.91K revenue stream across three core pillars: sales trends, product intelligence, and logistics performance 
providing the Executive Team with actionable solutions to reduce operational friction, reclaim revenue leakage, and optimize regional market share.

## Problem Statement
Northwind Traders had a global customer base and a active product rate, but the data revealed hidden inefficiencies.
Revenue leakage from aggressive discounting, an over-reliance on two markets, and an underperforming shipping partnership were quietly eroding profitability.
The core question: Is Northwind winning through brand demand or buying market share through discounts?

## Key Analysis Questions
1. Regional Market Dominance
2. Revenue Leakage from Discounting
3. Logistics & Shipping Efficiency
4. Discontinued Products tying up capital
